Our Story

Angoshree — Your Trusted
Saree Destination in Siliguri

Welcome to Angoshree, our saree shop in the heart of Siliguri, West Bengal. For the past few years, we've been helping customers find the perfect saree — whether it's for a wedding, festival, or everyday elegance.

From rich Bengali tant and silk to bridal reds and contemporary designs, we curate sarees that celebrate tradition while fitting modern tastes. Walk into our store and experience the fabrics firsthand.
